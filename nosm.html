Product: Virtual Safety Manager

Safety structure and guidance, without hiring a safety manager

The Virtual Safety Manager is built for organizations that don’t have dedicated safety leadership, but still need clear processes, consistent documentation, and accessible safety resources. Establish a strong safety foundation with guided workflows that help teams plan work safely and stay aligned.

Modules included

Core tools to help teams plan safely, access documentation fast, and get safety guidance when questions arise.

Job Hazard Analysis (JHA)

Guided pre-task planning to help teams identify hazards and document controls before starting work.

  • Standard templates for repeatable job planning
  • Clear documentation of hazards and controls
  • Consistency, even without safety expertise on staff

Safety Plan

Upload, manage, and distribute safety plans so crews always know the current procedures and expectations.

  • Central access to the latest safety requirements
  • Supports consistency across teams and sites
  • Reduces reliance on tribal knowledge

Safety Portal

A single source of truth for your safety documentation, policies, references, and company-specific materials.

  • Searchable, centralized safety resources
  • Improves field access and awareness
  • Reduces confusion and outdated documents

ASK Safety

Give teams a way to ask safety questions and get routed guidance for clarification and support (non-emergency).

  • Improves communication between field and office
  • Encourages “ask before you act” behavior
  • Helps teams make safer decisions in real time
Note: This product is ideal for companies that want to build a formal safety program and consistent documentation practices, but don’t have a dedicated safety manager today.
