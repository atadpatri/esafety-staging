Frequently asked questions

Short answers that help buyers and safety leaders evaluate fit quickly.

Is eSafety mobile-friendly for field teams?

Yes, modules are designed for fast, structured field capture so supervisors and crews can report, inspect, and document from the jobsite.

Can we customize forms and workflows?

Yes, inspection checklists, JHAs, toolbox talks, and other workflows can be tailored to your processes and job types.

How does eSafety support compliance?

eSafety standardizes documentation, supports OSHA reporting workflows, and centralizes records so your program stays consistent and audit-ready.
